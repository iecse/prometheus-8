ABOUT US

Whenever an empire ends, a new one takes it's place to fill the void. This is exactly what happened after the cryptocrash of 20'. The cashless world fell on its knees, the world economy crumbled, lawlessness spread and the Syndicate rose to the occasion. Syndicate is a group of programmers, economists, sociologists, and scientists at the top of their games granting us the capability to lead the world through these times of turmoil to an era of stability and prosperity.
