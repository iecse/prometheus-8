ABOUT US

The world went cashless. THE SYNDICATE, a hacker organization caused a massive cryptocurrency failure. Banks were shut down, dictatorship was enforced and the world was on the edge of chaos. After suffering for 10-15 years, the rebels decided to take action. A dystopian society where one group of individuals are enforcing their perspective of a better tomorrow and another set of individuals trying to fight back the oppression. From money laundering and gambling on one side to massive missile attacks being planned on the other, the world is in a state of fallout. The printing press is on its toes with news articles flushing out every hour. The Syndicate is trying its best to recruit more individuals and is also forcing people to work for them. While the world is in chaos, there are still those who are neither on the good side nor the bad and just want to enjoy the world as it is. But those who care for the world to fall into place, wish for their last hope, their savior, to come and help them during their fight for redemption. But be it the rebels or The Syndicate, everyone is just waiting for a new horizon.
